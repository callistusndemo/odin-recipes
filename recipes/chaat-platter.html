Mumbai Street Chaat Platter

This Mumbai Street Chaat Platter captures the vibrant and bustling flavors of Indian street food, reminiscent of the scenes from "Slumdog Millionaire." Enjoy the mix of textures and flavors as you savor each bite of these delicious chaat snacks!

Ingredients

  • Pani Puri shells
  • Bhel Puri mixture
  • Sev Puri shells
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Mint-coriander chutney

Steps

  1. Arrange the Pani Puri shells, Bhel Puri mixture, and Sev Puri shells on a serving platter.
  2. Fill the Pani Puri shells with a spoonful of Bhel Puri mixture.
  3. Top the Sev Puri shells with a generous amount of sev and chopped onions.
  4. Serve the chaat platter with small bowls of tamarind chutney and mint-coriander chutney on the side.
